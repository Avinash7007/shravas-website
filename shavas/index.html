Who We Are

Precision-Driven Consultancy for the Textile Industry

Shravas Consultancy & Services is a specialised quality assurance and merchandising consultancy headquartered in Noida, India. We partner with manufacturers, exporters, and retailers across the apparel and home textiles sector to deliver measurable improvements in quality, process efficiency, and supply chain performance.

  • Expert-led Quality Assurance across the entire production lifecycle
  • Lean & Six Sigma consulting to eliminate waste and reduce defects
  • Third-party inspection services trusted by global buyers and brands
  • Comprehensive factory technical evaluation and compliance audits
🎯

Quality Assurance & Merchandising

End-to-end quality oversight from raw material inspection to final shipment, ensuring every product meets buyer specifications and international standards.

📐

Lean & Six Sigma Consulting

Data-driven process optimisation methodologies to reduce defects, improve throughput, and create sustainable operational excellence in your facilities.

What We Offer

Our Core Services

From sourcing and merchandising to rigorous factory assessments — we cover every critical touchpoint in your supply chain.

🔍

Quality Assurance

Comprehensive in-line and final inspection services. We identify defects early and ensure your product meets all required quality standards before shipment.

🧵

Merchandising Services

Expert merchandising support from order placement through delivery — managing timelines, sampling, approvals, and production follow-up with precision.

📦

Sourcing

Strategic sourcing of fabrics, trims, and finished goods. We identify reliable suppliers that align with your quality expectations and cost targets.

🏭

Factory Technical Evaluation

In-depth technical assessments of manufacturing facilities evaluating infrastructure, machinery, workforce capability, and process compliance.

✅

Third Party Inspection

Independent, unbiased inspection services at every production stage — providing buyers with objective, reliable quality reports and risk assessments.

📊

Lean & Six Sigma

Structured consulting programs applying lean manufacturing and Six Sigma tools to drive waste reduction, improve quality yields, and boost productivity.
